Research Steps

Dr. Richard Demy began this project in 2013 by creating software that will guide his students through a series of practice strategies 

1

Testing Phase 

We have spent years working with students using the Beta version.  We have changed the values of variables for the app to make it more appealing and still effective.
2

Trials

We had a soft release in 2015 and learned many valuble lessons.  We had 1,000 users testing the application, and make many changes to meet their needs and input.
3

Lite Release 

This new release will only be available on Android and Windows. Feedback and use case is our number one priority, so let us know how well this worked for you.  
4

Future plans

Very shortly we will be releasing the lite version on Mac, iOS and other platforms.  We have a list of additional features we are working on but would like to get your feedback first!
